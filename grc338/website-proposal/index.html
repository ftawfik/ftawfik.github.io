Introduction

Fomo Fits

Fomo Fits is a clothing brand company started in my college apartment in 2022. Julian Gregorio, my roomate is a co-founder of the company. Our goal is to create uniqe designs that people can wear in public and turn heads.

Target audience

The people who shop on this site appreciate art and modern trends. It can be anybody that likes cool t-shirt designs they can wear in public to get noticed. These people usually buy from "hype" brands like Supreme and Gucci. Since the designs are diverse, the target is anyone who can relate to the brand.

The target's primary goal is to buy clothes that makes them look good and turns heads in public. Wearing Fomo is like making a statement to people, it's a status symbol.

Home

[Picture of someone standing in front of a beach wearing a graphic t-shirt.]

Welcome to Fomo Fits. Our goal is to create diverse, abstract, and creative designs for clothing that people can relate to. Fomo (Fear of missing out) is an emotion that everyone has inside of them, even if it's miniscule. These articles of clothing will emulate the Fomo expression.

About

Fomo Fits was co-founded by Fady Tawfik and Julian Gregorio (Juulz). Fady and Juulz started the company in March 2022 from their apartment building in San Luis Obispo. Juuls studies business and Fady studies Graphic Communication at California Polytechnic State University San Luis Obispo. Juulz had experience running a business through barbering and Fady had design and coding experience. Together they came together to create Fomo Fits.
